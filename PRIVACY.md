# Privacy Policy — PromptEnhancer

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Summary

PromptEnhancer does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. Period.

## What data does PromptEnhancer access?

- **Text you type in AI chat interfaces** — read locally to enhance your prompt. Never stored, never transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers).
- **Visible chat history** — read locally in AI mode only, sent to YOUR chosen AI provider using YOUR API key. Never sent anywhere else.
- **Persona and API settings** — stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`. Never leaves your device.

## What data does PromptEnhancer NOT access?

- Browsing history
- Cookies
- Passwords
- Personal files
- Any data outside of the active AI chat tab

## Third-party services

If you configure AI mode, your prompt text is sent to the AI provider YOU choose (Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Ollama). This is initiated by you and uses your own API key. PromptEnhancer has no relationship with these providers and receives no data from them.

## Data storage

All user settings are stored locally in your browser via `chrome.storage.local`. Uninstalling the extension deletes all stored data.

## Analytics and tracking

None. Zero. No analytics SDKs, no tracking pixels, no telemetry.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/avi-B-AI-Dev/prompt-enhancer/issues).
